William J. Brazelton

I am a scientist who studies natural microbial communities, specializing in metagenomic approaches. All of my data and code are open-source and publicly available here. I like to collaborate, so please reach out if you have new project ideas.

My research mostly falls within two general topics. Click on each of these to read more:

Rock-Powered Life
Explorations of microbes that live in subsurface rocks, with implications for the origin of life and astrobiology.
Antibiotic Resistance
Metagenomic approaches to monitor environmental reservoirs of antibiotic resistance.
